## Findings
### Franciscan e-Care (School ERP)
- URL: https://www.franciscansolutions.com/erp.aspx
- Main Modules Found:
  - **Online Admission**: Streamlines inquiry, application, enrollment, registration, customized forms, integrated online payments.
  - **Fee Management**: Automates fee collection, receipt generation, multiple payment modes (online/offline), fine calculation, concession management, and pending fee alerts.
  - **Payroll Management**: Manages employee profiles, attendance integration, salary calculation, loan/advance tracking, statutory compliance (PF, ESI, TDS), and payslip generation.
  - **Accounts Management**: Comprehensive financial accounting, ledger management, trial balance, P&L, balance sheet, voucher entry, and audit trails.
  - **Marks Management**: Automates grade/GPA calculation, customized report cards, multivariate analysis, class-wise reports, integrated SMS for grades, and board result performance analysis.
  - **Library Management**: Dynamic book cataloging, barcode integration, member management, issuance/return tracking, fine management, and shelf management.
  - **Time Management**: Automated timetable generation, substitution management, period-wise tracking, teacher workload optimization, and classroom allocation.
  - **Attendance Management**: Multi-mode attendance (manual, RFID, Biometric), real-time parent alerts (SMS/App), leave management, and daily/monthly reporting.
  - **Stock Management**: Inventory tracking, purchase order management, vendor management, low-stock alerts, and material issuance to staff/departments.
  - **Infirmary Management**: Student health records, medication tracking, illness history, vaccination records, and emergency contact integration.
  - **Seating Management**: AI-enabled exam seating layout generation (class/gender/group based), invigilator assignment, substitution management, and stationery tracking.
